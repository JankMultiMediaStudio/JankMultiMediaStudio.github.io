Then I'll produce that work, based on your input, in the corresponding time frame. The longer time frame you choose, the more detail, quality, and or duration the resulting commission will have. When its done I'll give you a copy of the work for no additional cost (unless you want something physically produced/shipped). I reserve full rights to reproduce and sell copies of the product, that way I can keep the commission prices low, otherwise we can talk price for the redistribution rights. Also I can't do anything involving anyone else's copyrighted or patented materials.
